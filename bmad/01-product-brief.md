# Product Brief — AI UMKM Growth Agent OS

## Status

Draft. Product name is not finalized.

## One-Line Vision

Build an AI-powered SaaS that gives each UMKM a visible daily-working growth team: Sales Agent, Marketing Agent, Business Strategy Agent, Capital Readiness Agent, and IT Agent.

## Problem

Many UMKM tools stop at helping owners create websites, post content, or run ads. Those are useful, but they do not guarantee that the owner receives actionable selling opportunities every day.

Typical UMKM pain points:

- Dependence on paid ads without clear ROI.
- Website exists but does not create enough leads.
- Social media content exists but does not consistently create sales conversations.
- Leads from WhatsApp, forms, referrals, and social media are not tracked properly.
- Follow-up is inconsistent.
- Quotation, order, payment, and customer history are scattered.
- Owner does not know what to do every day to grow sales.
- Business data is not structured enough for financing or investor/capital readiness.

## Product Thesis

UMKM owners need a system that creates sales opportunities, guides execution, tracks progress, and measures impact.

The product should feel like an agent that works every day, not like passive software waiting for the owner to operate it.

## Core Promise

The system helps business owners:

1. Register and structure their products/services.
2. Generate website and landing pages.
3. Discover relevant customer opportunities through safe and compliant prospecting.
4. Create daily to-do lists based on proven sales and marketing playbooks.
5. Guide outreach and follow-up.
6. Help prepare quotations and sales materials.
7. Track leads, prospects, tasks, follow-ups, pipeline, orders, and revenue.
8. Recommend business strategy improvements.
9. Support capital/investor readiness by organizing business data and documents.
10. Show live reports so the owner can see daily work and progress.

## What Makes It Different

Traditional UMKM SaaS:

- Builds website.
- Schedules social media content.
- Provides generic CRM or POS.
- Shows passive analytics.

This product:

- Creates daily sales tasks.
- Searches for relevant opportunities.
- Explains why each prospect is relevant.
- Helps owners act through guided playbooks.
- Shows live task progress.
- Links every activity to measurable sales outcomes.
- Stores every action and result in a database for learning and improvement.

## Target Users

Initial target is not all UMKM.

Best-fit users are UMKM that sell products/services through a consultative or semi-custom sales process:

- Jersey/custom apparel
- Printing and packaging
- Plastic molding/custom manufacturing
- Furniture custom
- Akrilik/signage
- Catering/event services
- Bengkel/service businesses
- Supplier B2B local businesses
- Konveksi
- Souvenir custom
- Interior/custom build services

Common pattern:

```text
Potential customer has a need
→ asks questions
→ needs recommendation/quotation
→ owner follows up
→ customer pays DP/full payment
→ order is fulfilled
→ customer may repeat order
```

## Non-Goals

The product should not become:

- A spam engine.
- A tool for mass cold WhatsApp blasting.
- A generic website builder.
- A generic content generator with no sales connection.
- A generic CRM that only stores contacts.
- A promise machine claiming guaranteed sales without qualification and execution.

## Success Philosophy

The product should not claim that every business will automatically become successful.

Instead, success should be defined through measurable progress:

- More qualified prospects discovered.
- More leads captured.
- More follow-up actions completed.
- More quotations sent.
- Higher pipeline value.
- More deals/orders won.
- Better repeat order activation.
- Clearer break-even and ROI tracking.
- Better capital readiness.

## Business Model Direction

Subscription pricing is not fixed.

Pricing must be calculated based on:

- AI/token usage.
- Server and database cost.
- Website hosting cost.
- Storage and bandwidth.
- Prospect research cost.
- Email/notification/WhatsApp integration cost.
- Human support/onboarding cost.
- Compliance and data verification cost.
- Desired gross margin.
- Merchant ROI threshold.

A sample monthly price may be used for discussion, but the final pricing must come from a unit economics model.
